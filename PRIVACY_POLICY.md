# Privacy Policy for Clarity Blocker

**Last Updated:** February 14, 2026

## Overview

Clarity Blocker is a Chrome extension designed to help you block distracting adult content websites. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Store (Locally Only)

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API. We do NOT send any data to external servers.

The following data is stored on your device:

- Your streak timer (start time, running status, last streak duration)
- Custom blocked websites you add
- Custom Reddit subreddits you want blocked
- Your image category preference (cute animals, man encouraging, woman encouraging, anime art, Christian mode)
- Blocker enabled/disabled status
- Cached OISD blocklist (updated daily from public source)
- Your product recommendation display preference

### What We DO NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

- Your browsing history
- The specific sites you visit or try to visit
- Your personal information (name, email, etc.)
- Your location
- Any analytics or tracking data
- Any identifiable information

### Chrome Sync (Optional)

If you have Chrome Sync enabled in your browser settings:

- Your extension settings may sync across your Chrome browsers
- This is handled entirely by Google's Chrome Sync service
- We do not have access to your synced data
- You can disable Chrome Sync in Chrome settings at any time

## Third-Party Services

### 1. Pexels API

We use the Pexels API (https://www.pexels.com) to display motivational images on blocked pages.

- **Data sent:** Anonymous API request for random images based on your selected category
- **No personal data sent**
- Pexels Privacy Policy: https://www.pexels.com/privacy-policy/

### 2. Nekos.best API

If you enable anime mode, we use Nekos.best API for anime images.

- **Data sent:** Anonymous API request for random anime images
- **No personal data sent**

### 3. OISD Blocklist

We download a public blocklist from OISD (https://nsfw.oisd.nl) once per day.

- This is a publicly available list of adult content domains
- The list is cached locally on your device
- **No personal data sent**

### 4. Amazon Associates Program

We participate in the Amazon Associates Program and may display affiliate links to recommended products.

**What this means:**

- We earn a small commission if you purchase products through our affiliate links
- We do NOT track which links you click
- We do NOT know what you purchase
- Amazon may use cookies to track affiliate referrals (governed by Amazon's privacy policy)
- **Disclosure:** As an Amazon Associate, Clarity Blocker earns from qualifying purchases

You are not required to use these links. All links open in a new tab and clearly indicate they are product recommendations.

Amazon Privacy Policy: https://www.amazon.com/privacy

## How We Use Data

Your locally stored data is used exclusively to:

- Track your streak progress
- Block websites you've specified
- Display your preferred motivational image category
- Remember your settings between browsing sessions

We do NOT:

- Send your data to any servers we control
- Share your data with third parties (except the third-party services listed above, which receive no personal data)
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising or analytics

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data is stored locally on your device indefinitely until you delete it
- You can delete all extension data at any time using the "Clear All Data" button in Settings
- Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data
- You can export your settings using the "Export Settings" feature before deletion

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **View** your data in the extension Settings
- **Delete** all data using the "Clear All Data" button in Settings
- **Export** your settings using the "Export Settings" button
- **Disable** the extension at any time from Chrome's extension settings
- **Uninstall** the extension to permanently remove all data

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Security

All data is stored using Chrome's built-in secure storage API. Since data never leaves your device (except for anonymous API calls to third-party services), there is minimal security risk.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

- The "Last Updated" date at the top will change
- Significant changes will be noted in the Chrome Web Store listing
- Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance

## Legal Compliance

### GDPR (European Union)

If you are in the EU:

- **Legal basis:** Legitimate interest (providing blocking functionality) and consent (optional features)
- **Your rights:** Access, deletion, portability, objection
- **Data location:** All data stored locally on your device
- **Data controller:** The extension developer

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control and can exercise all rights directly through the extension settings.

### CCPA (California)

If you are in California:

- We do NOT sell your personal information
- We do NOT share your personal information with third parties for marketing
- You can delete your data at any time using extension settings

## Contact

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy:

- **Support:** Contact through Chrome Web Store support page
- **Data deletion requests:** Use the "Clear All Data" button in Settings (immediate effect)
